Laura Gallisá is a design leader & front-end developer.

Laura is the Head of Design at Material Security.

Previously, she led a design team at Apple through the launch of Apple Business Essentials. Before that, she was Head of Design at Fleetsmith (acquired by Apple), designed & developed experiences at Dropbox, and was a founding member of the web design systems and styles team at Twitch.

Laura received her B.S. in Computer Science from NYU Tandon School of Engineering while she worked at Tendigi, a design agency in Brooklyn. Laura grew up on Long Island, NY, where she ate bagels, played Zelda, and went to the beach.
